“What is realized in my history is neither the past definite as what was, since it is no more, nor even the perfect as what has been in what I am, but the future anterior as what I will have been, given what I am in the process of becoming.”

Jacques Lacan, The Function and Field of Speech and Language in Psychoanalysis (1953)

This website is a number of things: a personal archive/showcase, a blog, an amalgamation of content from various fields, a digital garden, and lastly a place that promotes libre/free software with all its goodies.

The name of the domain and website is a direct implication towards my affiliation to the study/practice of psychoanalysis. And as such, it forms the spirit of almost all things that appear here, regardless of whatever place or field they come from.
