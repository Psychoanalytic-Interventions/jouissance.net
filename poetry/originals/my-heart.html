My Heart

This was the very second poem I had written, and probably the one closest to me.

~My Heart~

What am I afraid of? My heart, the fountain of misery.

It fails me, betraying me of myself.

Only to incure more of melancholy.

It brings love, during moments rought

Hah, my sweet heart:

Isn’t love just a labyrinth of anxiety?

It brings hope, to eyes sore.

Oh, bless those eyes,

Will they ever again be sleepy?

I still go on, as a system of scarsl

And in the jungle of memories,

I venture the labyrinths each ight,

Struggling to count the stars.

				-Divya Ranjan, 15th June 2022.
